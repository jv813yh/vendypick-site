About the game

One tap decides who's up next

VendyPick turns any get-together into a game. Everyone puts a finger on their assigned spot, VendyPick randomly picks one player, and the group gets a light, fun Truth or Dare style prompt to act on. No setup, no scorekeeping by hand — just pass the phone and play.

Built for the room you're already in. Gather 2 to 10 players, pick your question categories, choose how many rounds (3, 5, 7 or 10) and lives (1 to 3) you want, then enter everyone's name. Each round VendyPick reveals a Truth or Dare style prompt, the group scores it, and players earn stars — score zero and you lose a life, score one or two and the stars stack up. Everything happens locally on your device, with no internet connection needed, so it's perfect for road trips, waiting rooms, and living-room nights.

How a round works

Set up, claim your spot, play

Set up the game

Choose 2–10 players, your rounds and lives, and which question categories to include. Then type in everyone's name.

PartySafeKnowActionDeep

Verify positions

Each player taps their glowing spot on the screen to claim it, so the phone knows where every finger is before the round begins.

Play and score

VendyPick picks the winning position and reveals a Truth or Dare prompt. Score the round 0–2 stars — zero costs a life, one or two add up.

This Privacy Policy explains how VendyPick (the "app") handles information. VendyPick is designed as a simple, offline party game, and it is built to respect your privacy by default.

No account, no personal data

  • VendyPick does not require account registration.
  • VendyPick does not collect personal information.
  • VendyPick does not use advertising SDKs.
  • VendyPick does not use analytics SDKs.
  • VendyPick does not track users.
  • VendyPick does not sell or share user data.

Gameplay stays on your device

  • Gameplay happens locally on your device.
  • Player names entered in the game are used only during local gameplay and are not sent to any server.
  • The app may store temporary local gameplay state or settings on the device, if applicable, but this data stays on the device.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from children. Because VendyPick collects no personal information at all, no child data is gathered through normal use.

Clearing your data

Since data stays on your device, you can remove any locally stored gameplay state or settings at any time by uninstalling the app or by clearing the app's storage in your Android settings.
