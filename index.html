How it works

The whole game is one simple loop, repeated for as many rounds as you choose.

Place fingers

Each player claims a spot and holds a finger on the screen.

The app picks

A quick roulette lands on one random player.

Truth or Dare

That player gets a prompt from the categories you enabled.

Score the round

The group awards 0, 1 or 2 stars. Zero stars costs a life.

Play on

Later rounds pick automatically until someone is crowned.

Every round, a new challenge

One player is picked, gets a Truth or Dare prompt from the categories you enabled, and the group decides how it went.

  • ✓Truth or Dare — a fresh prompt every round, never repeated in the same game.
  • ✓Score together — the group awards 0, 1 or 2 stars.
  • ✓Stars & lives — 0 stars removes 1 life; 1 or 2 stars are added.
  • ✓Three jokers — use the Player Joker, Question Joker or Cat in the Sack when the moment calls for it.

Set up in seconds

Open the app, pick your settings, and you are playing. No sign-up, no tutorial.

  • ✓Players — anywhere from 2 to 10 on one phone.
  • ✓Rounds & lives — choose 3, 5, 7, 10, 15, 20, 25 or 30 rounds and 1–3 lives each.
  • ✓Categories — switch the question worlds on and off to match the room.
  • ✓Language — play in any of eight languages.

Privacy Policy

VendyPick is built to need nothing about you. Here is exactly what that means.

Effective date: 12 July 2026

The short version

VendyPick does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not track you. Everything happens on your device.

Information we collect

None. VendyPick does not ask you to register or sign in, and it does not collect names, contacts, location, device identifiers, or any other personal data.

Player names and gameplay

Any names you type and any game progress stay on your device for the duration of play. They are not uploaded, shared, or stored on any server.

Ads and analytics

The app contains no advertising SDKs and no analytics or tracking SDKs. It does not build a profile of you and does not share data with third parties.

Internet access

VendyPick plays fully offline and does not require an internet connection. This website is static and loads no third-party resources.

Children

VendyPick is designed as a family-safe game. Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children either.

Permissions

The game runs using only what it needs to display on screen and detect touches. It does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
